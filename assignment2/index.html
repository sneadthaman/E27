The Chef

My name is Sam and I have a strong passion for cooking, especially during the holidays. My favorite cuisines are Latin, Spanish, Caribbean, Italian and classic French. I derive a great deal of pleasure from planning and strategizing my meals generally based on what I am craving at the moment! Nothing beats going to the market, especially if I haven't eaten all day, and buying everything on the shelves. I can envision all of my favorite dishes whether it's jerk chicken, peruvian classics, italian comfort food, or just a good spicy curry. $300 later and I still managed to forget something.

This blog represents some of my favorite tried and true recipes that I have collected from various books and websites (plus a likkle variation from me) that I love preparing for my family.
